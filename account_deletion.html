Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. This page outlines the process for requesting deletion of your Arooma account and associated data.

Important:

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal information, order history, and preferences will be removed from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@arooma.com.ng with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. In your email, you must include the email address associated with your Arooma account
  3. You may optionally include your reason for deletion (this helps us improve our services)
  4. Our team will process your request within 5 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted

Email Template

You can copy and paste the template below:

To: support@arooma.com.ng
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Hello Arooma Support,

I would like to request the deletion of my Arooma account.

My account email: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]

Reason for deletion (optional): [YOUR REASON]

Please confirm once the deletion is complete.

Thank you.

What Happens Next

After you submit your request:

  • Our team will verify your account ownership
  • We will delete all your personal data from our active systems
  • You will receive a confirmation email at your account email address
  • Your data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently purged

Data Retention

Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Records required for financial or tax reporting purposes
  • Anonymized data that no longer identifies you
  • Information necessary to prevent fraud or future abuse
