Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, download our app from the App Store, open it, and tap on the "Sign Up" button. You can create an account using your email or continue with Apple.

How do I place an order?

Browse restaurants in your area, select items to add to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to select a delivery address and payment method to complete your order.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time from the Orders section of the app.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards and other local payment options available in your region.

How do I update my delivery address?

You can manage your saved addresses in the Profile section. You can also add a new address during checkout.
